Guidelines During Unlockdown Period

Guidelines to be followed during Un-Lockdown period.

Precautions

Things you should do
* Every day, wear a new mask (cloth one will do) in public places. More so when in the proximity of people.
* Always carry a hand santiser and a spare mask.
* Carry you own food and water bottle to oﬃce.
* Carpooling, usually three people in a regular car or ﬁve in an SUV. is okay, but don't use AC. Keep the windows open. None of the occupants should have any symptoms linked to Covid-19.
* A trained person with a handheld thermal scanner should take temperature at the oﬃce entrance. Everyone should use santiser before entering.
* Person manning the entrance should look out for anyone with symptons (cough, cold, throat, pain, breathing diﬃculty). If someone exhibits such symptoms, they should be referred to a nearby hospital.
* Use sanitiser after touching high-contact surfaces such as door handles, lift doors and buttons, railings, grab bars.
* Natural ventilation and lighting are recommended.

Things you should avoid
* Don't unnecessarily touch your nose, eyes and mouth.
* Avoid unnecessary movement in the oﬃce and visits to other stations and persons.
* Limit face-to-face interactions.
* Avoid sharing food and beverage in the oﬃce.
* No handshake or hugging
* Avoid public transport as far as possible. If inevitable ensure a distance of at least 1 metre from another person.
* Try to limit use of public restrooms. Wash your hands thoroughly.
* Don't use common towels in restrooms. Use disposable tissues and hand driers.
* Avoid rush hours and visits to crowded areas.
